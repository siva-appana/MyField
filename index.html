Robotic Fruit Counting Service Platform for Orchards and Farms

About

We are a team of engineers from Georgia Tech finding opportunities to help farms and orchards increase productivity and yield with robots and data about your field.

Mission

Our mission is to bring our accurate and reliable fruit-counting and yield estimation robots to your fields to provide you with data about your crops to make informed decisions about estimated labor, harvest, and sale to markets. The growing population relies and values your produce, and we would like to take some work off your plate!

It's Simple!

  • Ground-based robots traverse your field to scan trees and bushes, reliably scanning and counting your fruit.
  • Our technology processes the data and provides you with the charts you want to see on our platform.
  • With our service, you do not need to manage or operate any robots.
