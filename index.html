About MyField

An engineering senior design team from Georgia Tech hoping to make a splash in agriculture.

Mission

Our mission is to bring our accurate and reliable fruit-counting and yield estimation robots to your fields to provide you with data about your crops to make informed decisions about estimated labor, harvest, and sale to markets. The growing population relies and values your produce, and we would like to take some work off your plate!
